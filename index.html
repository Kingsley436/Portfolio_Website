ABOUT ME

My name is Kingsley Quarshie and I am twenty years of age. I come from Osu in the Greater Accra Region. I currently live at Kasoa which can be located in the Central Region of Ghana. I am a member of the Methodist Church Ghana and also a proud member of the Methodist Youth Fellowship (MYF).

I attended Advance Tech Computer Training Institute where I studied and graduated with a Diploma in Microsoft Office Suite and Graphic Design. I am run a business called Unique Multimedia where I undertake a lot of services under graphic designing. These services include; Digital Cartooning, Posters, Letter head, Citation, Banners, T-Shirt Designs, Brochures, Flyers and Logos.

I am currently a student of the nation's premier university which is the University of Ghana, Legon where I am studying Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with the sole aim of becoming a Web developer and a Programmer in the future. I am a resident of Commonwealth Hall which is the best hall on campus. The City on a hill where Truth Stands.
